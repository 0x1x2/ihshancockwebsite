Period 2 - Math Foundations

This class focuses on continuing to develop
number sense and teach problem-solving
strategies that are later applied to story problems
throughout the book. Concepts involving critical
thinking and problem solving are taught through
guided practice and application of math concepts
in everyday living situations.

Required Materials:

  • Click HERE for printable graph paper.
  • Scientific Calculator (if you need to purchase one, the TI-34
    MultiView is recommended. A graphing calculator is not
    required until Algebra 2 and above.)

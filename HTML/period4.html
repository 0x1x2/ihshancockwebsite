Course Overview:

Provides a structured class where students
receive study skills instruction and assistance
with assignments. Emphasis is placed on math,
reading and writing strategies used in general
education texts, application of basic writing
skills, the use of a planner and students
developing their self-advocacy skills necessary
for success in general education classes.

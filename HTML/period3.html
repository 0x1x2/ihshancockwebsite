Period 3 - Consumer Math

This course teaches real-life application of math skills that will be needed after graduation by using the AGS Consumer Math textbook. Students focus on improving their problem solving skills with the use of a calculator. This course builds skills needed for the Compass test (entrance exam to Bellevue College, Renton Technical College and the Lake Washington Institute of Technology).

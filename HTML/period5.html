Period 5 - Intro to Algebra 1

Traditional concepts of algebra that are required
for Algebra 1A will be introduced and reinforced
throughout the year. Using the TransMath
Understanding Algebraic Expressions, level 3 book,
emphasis will be on mastering single-step equations,
writing and solving proportions, converting between
fractions, decimals and percents, finding the mean,
median and mode, data analysis, area, perimeter and
surface area, order of operations, inequalities and
writing equations and inequalities from simple word
problems.
